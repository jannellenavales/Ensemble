I searched
for a place of peace,
And thought I found my calling Shivered through the midnight
breeze, In exchange
for belonging.

Crafting stories through movement,
While bonding with my sisters,
Brought a sense of fulfillment,
Too bad - it ended bitter.

I’m left with an artifact,
Of what could’ve been my home,
At least I’ve kept memos past,
To reflect on while alone.
